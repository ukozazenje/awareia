AWAREIA – A DENSO PARTNER

DENSO, part of the Toyota Group, is the inventor of the ubiquitous QR Code®. By leveraging DENSO’s innovative, patented SQRC® technology, Awareia’s subscribers are using the world’s only ISO/IEC18004 compliant 2D barcode with built-in, secure public and private action. Coupled with Awareia’s ingenuity, the result is an intelligent patent-pending, lost and found alert system that delivers an enhanced customer service experience for both subscribers and Awareia-enabled hotspots.
